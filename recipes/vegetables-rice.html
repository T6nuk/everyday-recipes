Vegetables with rice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice (white or brown)
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 carrot
  • half a bell pepper
  • half a zucchini
  • half a tsp of paprika
  • salt & pepper

How to make:

  1. Bring 2 cups of water (or broth) to a boil. Add the rice, lower the heat, cover, and cook as per package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil or butter over medium heat. Add garlic and cook for about 30 seconds.
  3. Stir in the carrot, bell pepper, and zucchini. Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add salt, pepper, turmeric or paprika, and soy sauce if using. Mix well.
  5. Add the cooked rice to the pan with the veggies. Stir everything together and cook for another 2 minutes. Enjoy!
